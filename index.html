Welcome to Healing Pathways Therapy

A compassionate, trauma-informed private practice dedicated to supporting your journey toward healing from complex trauma, PTSD, childhood wounds, and inner shadow work.

About Me

I am a licensed therapist with extensive experience in trauma-informed care. Specializing in EMDR, somatic experiencing, Internal Family Systems (IFS), and gentle shadow work integration, I create a safe, nurturing space for adults healing from complex PTSD, childhood trauma, and relational wounds.

My approach is rooted in empathy, neuroscience, and mindfulness, empowering you to process deep emotions, regulate your nervous system, and rediscover wholeness and resilience.

Services Offered

  • EMDR Therapy

    Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing to gently process and release traumatic memories.

  • Somatic Experiencing

    Body-centered therapy to release stored trauma and restore natural nervous system balance.

  • Internal Family Systems (IFS)

    Compassionate exploration and harmonizing of inner parts affected by trauma.

  • Shadow Work Integration

    Gentle illumination of unconscious patterns for deeper self-acceptance and growth.

  • Complex PTSD Support

    Tailored strategies for managing chronic trauma symptoms and building stability.

  • Childhood Trauma Healing

    Specialized care for resolving adverse childhood experiences and fostering secure inner attachments.

My Trauma-Informed Approach

Every session prioritizes safety, trust, choice, and empowerment. By blending EMDR for efficient trauma processing, somatic techniques for body awareness, IFS for inner harmony, and mindful shadow work, we collaboratively address the roots of pain while nurturing your innate capacity for healing and growth.

Together, we move at your pace toward greater peace, self-compassion, and authentic living.
