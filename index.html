About Me

I am a licensed therapist specializing in trauma-informed care, EMDR, Somatic Experiencing, and Internal Family Systems.

My work centers on nervous-system regulation, safety, and compassionate integration of difficult life experiences.

  • Somatic Experiencing

    Releasing trauma stored in the body.

  • Internal Family Systems

    Healing wounded inner parts with compassion.

  • Shadow Work

    Exploring unconscious patterns safely.

  • Complex PTSD

    Stabilization, regulation, and integration.

  • Childhood Trauma

    Repairing early attachment wounds.
